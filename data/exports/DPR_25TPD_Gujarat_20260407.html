3. Technology

Pyrolysis of agricultural biomass at 450-550 degrees C in oxygen-free reactor.
Yields: Bio-Oil 32% | Bio-Char 28% | Syngas 22% | Loss 18%
Blending: 20% bio-oil with VG30 conventional bitumen
Standard: CSIR-CRRI licensed, meets IS:73 and MoRTH 2026 Section 519.
